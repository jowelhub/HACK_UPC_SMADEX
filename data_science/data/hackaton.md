## Smadex - Creative Intelligence Challenge

### Background

Mobile advertisers launch many ad creatives across multiple campaigns, countries and devices. One of the hardest parts of running campaigns is understanding **which creatives are working, why they are working, and when they start losing effectiveness**.

At Smadex, we want to make this process easier.

### The Challenge

Build a tool that helps advertisers analyze ad creatives and make better creative decisions.

Using the dataset provided by Smadex, your solution should help answer questions such as:

- Which creatives are the top performers?
- What characteristics seem to be associated with better performance?
- Which creatives are showing signs of fatigue over time?
- What should the advertiser test next?

Your goal is not to build a perfect prediction model. Your goal is to build a **useful and explainable prototype** that helps a marketer understand creative performance and take action.

### What You Will Get

You will receive a synthetic but realistic dataset that includes:

- advertisers
- campaigns
- creatives
- daily performance data
- creative images/assets
- metadata such as country, OS, format, vertical and performance KPIs

This is enough to build anything from a simple analytics dashboard to an AI-powered creative copilot.

### What Your Solution Should Do

Your prototype should include **at least two** of the following capabilities:

1. **Creative Performance Explorer**
Show which creatives perform best across different dimensions such as campaign, country, OS or format.
2. **Creative Fatigue Detection**
Identify creatives that appear to be losing performance over time.
3. **Explainability Layer**
Provide insights or hypotheses about why a creative is performing well or poorly.
4. **Recommendation Engine**
Suggest which creative to scale, pause, or test next.
5. **Creative Similarity / Clustering**
Group similar creatives and compare how they perform.

### Expected Output

You can build any kind of prototype, for example:

- a web app
- a dashboard
- an AI assistant
- a notebook with a strong interactive demo
- a lightweight decision-support tool

The final demo should clearly show:

- the problem you are solving
- how your solution works
- what insight or decision an advertiser gets from it

### Constraints

- You do **not** need prior ad-tech knowledge.
- You do **not** need to build a production-ready system.
- You may use rules, heuristics, ML, LLMs, computer vision, or a combination of approaches.
- Explainability and usability matter more than complexity.

### Evaluation Criteria

Projects will be judged on:

- **Usefulness:** Does it help an advertiser make better decisions?
- **Clarity:** Are the insights easy to understand?
- **Technical Quality:** Is the solution well built?
- **Creativity:** Is the approach original or especially clever?
- **Demo Quality:** Is the final prototype convincing and easy to follow?

### Bonus Points

We will especially like solutions that:

- combine image understanding with performance data
- detect patterns across many creatives automatically
- generate clear next-step recommendations
- make complex data easy to understand for non-technical users

### In One Sentence

Build the next creative copilot for mobile advertisers.
